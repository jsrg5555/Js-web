About Us

Established in 2018, GM SALES - Decorite Beauty & Salon Solutions is engrossed for Manufacturing, Designing Wholesale Trading & Supplying an attractive wide range of Professional Styling Salon Chair, Backwash Unit, Shampoo Station, Facial Bed, Spa Massage Bed, Pedicure Manicure Station – Jacuzzi Tub, Hair & Facial Trolley's, Wooden Trolley Cart, Designer Fiber- Wooden or Working Stools and All Type Salon Accessory & Equipment's with Office Furniture etc.

We were work on highest quality of Modern and young designs, functionality and quality with affordable prices or customization, as per Client requirement or Layout plan. Like Color combination theme is our USP. Our products range made with best raw material such as stainless steel, leather, foams, plastic, galvanized copper, are appreciated for their elegant appearance, easy portability, comfort and long service life.

Besides we are using high-end technology in tandem with industry-set guidelines to fabricate the products. We are also offering customized products, catering to varying needs of clients that include top Beauty Salon, Spas, Hotels, Resorts, Makeup Studios, Hair & Beauty Institutes and family unisex Salons across the Nation. We are Design All Purpose Aesthetics Beauty Beds which can widely used in Unisex Salon or Beauty Parlour, Spas & Massage Centre, Tattoo Shops, Laser Removal and Skin Treatment Clinics by Dermatology Surgeons.

"DECORITE" is born as developing brand in the Beauty Salon & Spa Furniture Market, Thanks to its values perfectly shown with fresh-looking products. Modern and young design, functionality and quality with affordable prices are the Decorite inspiration values, which bring the company to an important Market growth. “ Every day we work with passion to optimize your salon and support the hairdresser professionalism”
